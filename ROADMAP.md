## 🧭 Guiding Principles

1. **Evergreen over trending.** Trending dies; evergreen (finance, health, tech, psychology, history, self-improvement) pays for years.
2. **CTR is king, AVD is queen, retention is the kingdom.**
3. **Every video is an ad for the next video.** End screens + playlists > viral hope.
4. **Hire before you drown.** Hire slow, fire fast, document everything (that's why this repo exists).
5. **Data > guru advice.** If the analytics say a topic works, make 10 more on it.
6. **Repurpose relentlessly.** 1 long form → 5 Shorts → 1 newsletter → 10 social posts.
