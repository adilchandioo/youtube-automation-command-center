# SOP-001: Daily Publish Routine

**Version**: 1.0
**Owner**: Uploader VA (final approval: Channel Manager / Founder)
**Frequency**: Every day, 2 hours before scheduled publish time
**Goal**: Ensure 1–2 long-form videos + 2 Shorts publish on schedule, zero mistakes.

---

## 1. Morning Check (9:00 AM, audience-local time)

- [ ] Open Trello/Notion pipeline board
- [ ] Confirm "🚀 Scheduled" list has at least 3 videos ready for next 3 days
- [ ] Check YouTube Studio → Content tab: no strikes, no copyright claims from yesterday
- [ ] Check email: any YouTube notifications? Respond to critical only (strikes, claims)
- [ ] Check Dashboard: any videos spiking (double views vs 7d avg)? → flag for Shorts clipping

## 2. Today's Video QA (before publishing)

For every video scheduled today:
- [ ] Open the YouTube scheduled entry
- [ ] **Watch the FIRST 60 seconds** of rendered video — check audio levels, captions, black frames
- [ ] **Scrub through** middle and end — check transitions, B-roll sync, no watermarks from tools
- [ ] Confirm thumbnail loads and looks good on mobile preview
- [ ] Confirm title is <70 chars, hooky, keyword at front
- [ ] Confirm description has: 0:00 timestamps, affiliate disclosures, subscribe CTA
- [ ] Confirm tags are filled (10–15 tags)
- [ ] Confirm end screen & cards are set
- [ ] Confirm playlist assigned
- [ ] Confirm visibility is "Scheduled" for correct time (not Private/Public by accident)

## 3. Shorts Upload (if on schedule)

- [ ] Open CapCut project with Shorts clips (vertical 9:16)
- [ ] Export 1080x1920, 30fps
- [ ] Upload to YouTube:
  - Title: hook in ALL CAPS or question format (use Shorts title formula in templates)
  - Description: "#shorts" + 1-sentence + "Watch full video: [link]"
  - Tags: 5–7 tags including #shorts
  - Thumbnail: select a frame with face/emotion OR Canva thumbnail
  - **No end screen / cards on Shorts**
  - Schedule to publish 30 min before long-form (drives traffic to long form)
- [ ] Same Shorts cross-post to TikTok + Instagram Reels via Buffer/ manual upload

## 4. Post-Publish (15 min after it goes live)

- [ ] Verify video went Public (check YouTube Studio)
- [ ] Post a pinned comment: question to drive engagement + lead magnet plug
  - Example: "Which of these 5 side hustles are you going to try first? Drop it below 👇 And grab our free 30-side-hustles PDF — link in bio!"
- [ ] Send Discord/Slack ping to team: "🚀 LIVE: [Title] — [URL]"
- [ ] Move Trello card from "Scheduled" → "Published"
- [ ] Update KPI tracker with publish timestamp

## 5. Community Management (15 min)

- [ ] Reply to first 20–30 comments (use reply templates, or AI reply bot if enabled)
- [ ] Heart positive comments
- [ ] Pin one insightful comment from a viewer
- [ ] Delete/hide spam (crypto scams, "I make $500/day DMs" type)

## 6. End of Day Wrap (6:00 PM)

- [ ] Update daily stats in `analytics/kpi-trackers/daily-log.csv`
- [ ] Flag any videos underperforming at 24h (CTR < 4% or AVD < 30%)
- [ ] Ensure tomorrow's videos are fully QA'd and scheduled
- [ ] Backlog check: "Ready to Upload" must have ≥2 videos; if not, nudge editor/designer

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Forgetting affiliate disclosure — FTC violation; always put "#ad" or "affiliate link" in description
❌ Scheduling for wrong timezone — use audience analytics to find peak hour
❌ Publishing video with typo in title — triple-check
❌ Forgetting captions — 70%+ viewers watch without sound
❌ Music too loud over voice — aim for -20 to -24dB on music bed
❌ Using thumbnail on wrong channel — double-check branding
❌ Posting Shorts without #shorts tag — algorithm may not recognize as Short

## Escalation

If any of these happen, STOP and message Founder immediately:
- Copyright claim or strike
- Video blocked in any country
- Account warning
- Disapproved ad status on monetized channel
- Hacked account / unrecognized login
