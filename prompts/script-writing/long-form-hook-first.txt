## SCRIPT REQUIREMENTS:

### 1. OPENING HOOK (0-30 seconds) — MOST IMPORTANT PART
- Start with ONE of these hook types:
  a) SHOCKING NUMBER: "In 2026, a single AI side hustle made a 23-year-old $893,000 — and nobody's talking about it."
  b) CONTROVERSY: "Every guru is lying to you about passive income. Here's the ugly truth."
  c) CURIOSITY GAP: "I stumbled on this trick by accident... and it doubled my income in 30 days."
  d) STORY: "Last month, a guy messaged me saying he was broke. 14 days later he made his first $1,000. Here's exactly what he did."
  e) DEFIANCE: "Don't get a job in 2026. I'm dead serious."
- State the EXACT payoff ("By the end of this video you'll know...")
- Create a curiosity gap so they MUST keep watching
- DO NOT say "hey guys welcome back to the channel" — that's boring, kill it

### 2. INTRO (30-60 sec)
- Quick 1-sentence channel positioning ("On this channel we show you how to make money online without the BS")
- Soft CTA: "If that sounds like something you want, hit subscribe — I don't waste your time"
- Tease the BEST/most surprising thing coming up later

### 3. BODY (main content, ~70% of script)
- 3-5 main points, numbered
- For EACH point:
  - CLEAR CLAIM ("Side hustle #3 pays $500-800/week and you can start today")
  - EVIDENCE: Specific stat, screenshot reference, story, or example ("My friend Rahul started this 6 months ago and now makes $1,200/week")
  - SPECIFIC HOW-TO: Actually tell them how (don't be vague like "start a blog" — say "Go to upwork.com, create a profile focused on AI writing, set your rate at $25/hr, apply to these 3 job types...")
  - MINI-CONCLUSION / transition line
- EVERY 90 SECONDS insert a mini-hook to keep retention ("Wait — this next one? It's the side hustle I would do if I was starting over from zero.")
- AT THE 50% MARK: a mid-roll retention hook ("We're halfway through — and the last two are the ones that actually made me money. Don't leave now.")
- Add [VISUAL CUES IN BRACKETS] for the editor:
  [B-ROLL: person typing on laptop]
  [SCREEN RECORDING: Upwork job listings]
  [GRAPH: earnings chart]
  [ANIMATION: text on screen]
  [POP-UP: "Subscribe now"]

### 4. OUTRO & CTA (last 45-60 seconds)
- Quick recap ("Those are the 5 side hustles that actually work in 2026")
- Hard CTA: LIKE ("If you got value, hit that like button — it genuinely helps the channel")
- SUBSCRIBE + notification bell
- COMMENT PROMPT: Ask a specific question ("Which of these 5 are you trying this week? Drop it in the comments — I reply to every comment for the first hour.")
- AFFILIATE / PRODUCT PLUG if provided: {{AFFILIATE_CTA}}
- Tease next video ("On Wednesday I'm showing you the AI tool that replaces 5 full-time employees — you don't want to miss that one. Subscribe so you don't.")

## WRITING RULES:
- Conversational, like talking to a friend
- NEVER use jargon without explaining it
- Short sentences. Fragments OK. Like this.
- Use "you" constantly (not "people" or "one")
- Contractions are fine ("don't", "can't", "won't")
- Avoid passive voice ("X was done by Y" → "Y did X")
- Use specific numbers ("7 out of 10", "earns $847/week", not "lots" or "a lot")
- Add at least ONE controversial or unexpected take
- DO NOT use generic openings ("In today's video...", "Welcome to our channel...")
- DO NOT end sections with "and that's it" or "hope you enjoyed" — keep moving forward
- NO filler. Every sentence must teach or hook.
- Write 150 words per minute of target runtime.
