# PROMPT: Thumbnail Design Brief (for Midjourney/DALL-E AND designer)

---

## PART 1 — AI Image Generation Prompt (for Midjourney / DALL-E)

Use this to generate the main subject of the thumbnail. Copy and adapt:

```
YouTube thumbnail image, {{STYLE}} style.
A {{SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}} with an EXTREME EMOTION of {{EMOTION}} — wide eyes, raised eyebrows, open mouth, pointing at camera.
{{CONTEXT_OBJECTS}} in background.
Lighting: bright, high contrast, studio lighting on face.
Colors: dominated by {{PRIMARY_COLOR}} and {{ACCENT_COLOR}}, dark background.
Composition: tight close-up on face, subject taking 60% of frame, looking slightly to the right.
Style: realistic photography, vibrant saturation, sharp focus, shallow depth of field.
NO extra text, NO logos, NO watermarks, NO multiple hands, NO extra fingers.
--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.0 --stylize 300
```

### Variables explained:
- {{STYLE}}: "cinematic", "hyperrealistic", "YouTube reaction face", "animated"
- {{SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}}: e.g., "30-year-old man in black hoodie", "businesswoman in blazer", "curious person"
- {{EMOTION}}: "shock/disbelief", "excitement/amazement", "confusion", "fear", "joy"
- {{CONTEXT_OBJECTS}}: e.g., "laptop showing money numbers, floating cash", "AI robot", "stock chart going up"
- {{PRIMARY_COLOR}} / {{ACCENT_COLOR}}: match channel brand colors (e.g., "#10b981 emerald green", "#f59e0b amber")

## PART 2 — Designer Brief (Canva/Photoshop composition)

After AI generates the face/object, send this to your designer:

```
Please create 2 thumbnail variants for this video.

CHANNEL: {{CHANNEL_NAME}}
Brand fonts: {{FONT_NAME}} (e.g., Bebas Neue / Impact)
Brand colors: Primary {{PRIMARY_COLOR}}, Accent {{ACCENT_COLOR}}, Background {{BG_COLOR}}

VIDEO TITLE: {{TITLE}}
VIDEO TOPIC: {{TOPIC}}

VARIANT A (emotion/face focused):
- Use the attached AI-generated face as the dominant element (60-70% of frame)
- Add 1-3 words of big text MAX (example: "I GOT BANNED", "$893,000", "DON'T DO THIS")
- Text in bold {{FONT_NAME}}, with thick black outline (4-6px) so it pops
- Add arrow/red circle pointing at key element if needed
- Background: slightly blurred B-roll from topic OR solid dark gradient with brand accent
- Overall feel: shocking, curiosity-inducing

VARIANT B (text/object focused):
- Large text (3 words max) dominant, with one supporting object (e.g., big dollar sign, screenshot, arrow)
- Use split layout — left side object, right side text (or vice versa)
- High contrast, bright colors
- More text-heavy but still readable at tiny mobile size
- Same fonts/colors as Variant A

SPECIFICATIONS:
- 1280x720px, export as PNG or JPG, <2MB
- Test at 10% zoom — must still read the text and identify the subject
- No spoilers (don't reveal the ending/payoff)
- No logos from competing brands
- Look at top 3 competitor videos on this topic — do SOMETHING different so we don't blend in

DELIVER: Two PNG files named CH-001-epXXX-thumb-a.png and CH-001-epXXX-thumb-b.png

Reference thumbnails I like:
{{REFERENCE_THUMBNAIL_LINKS_OR_DESCRIPTION}}
```

## PART 3 — Title & Thumbnail Pairing Check

Title and thumbnail must work TOGETHER as a curiosity machine:

| If title is... | Thumbnail should... |
|---|---|
| Number-heavy ("5 Ways to X") | Show big number + face reaction |
| Question ("Why is X banned?") | Show confused/shocked face + the X object |
| Shocking statement ("I got fired for X") | Show frustrated/sad face + context object |
| Tutorial ("How to X in 5 min") | Show step/result screenshot + finger pointing |
| Story ("How I went from 0 to $1M") | Before/after split OR shocked face with money |

Avoid: thumbnail SPOILING what the title is teasing (curiosity gap destroyed).
Avoid: thumbnail emotion CONTRADICTING title tone (title says "terrifying mistake" but thumbnail shows smiling face).
