## Experiment Ideas to Run

### Thumbnails
- [ ] Face vs no-face
- [ ] Red arrows/circles vs clean
- [ ] Bright yellow vs dark moody
- [ ] 1 word vs 3 words
- [ ] AI-generated vs stock photo faces

### Titles
- [ ] Number vs question vs statement
- [ ] Year in title vs no year
- [ ] ALL CAPS power word vs no caps
- [ ] Negative framing ("never do X") vs positive ("do X for best results")
- [ ] Parentheses "(#3 shocked me)" vs no parens

### Script/Hook
- [ ] Story-first hook vs number-first vs question-first
- [ ] Pace: 140 wpm vs 160 wpm vs 180 wpm
- [ ] Joke/humor at start vs serious/direct
- [ ] Subscribe ask early (30s) vs late (end) vs none

### Publishing
- [ ] Upload time: morning vs evening
- [ ] Upload day: Mon vs Wed vs Fri vs weekend
- [ ] Upload frequency: 1x/wk vs 3x/wk vs daily
- [ ] Premiere vs schedule silently
- [ ] Community post tease vs no tease

### Shorts
- [ ] Captions centered vs bottom third
- [ ] Trending audio vs voice-only
- [ ] End CTA "subscribe" vs "watch full video" vs "follow"
- [ ] 15 sec vs 45 sec Shorts
- [ ] Face vs screen-record only

---

**Rule**: Only run ONE major experiment at a time per channel. Otherwise you won't know what caused the change.
