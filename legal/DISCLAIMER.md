## Best Practices

1. Put these disclaimers in the **description** of every relevant video (don't just bury in a channel-wide page — YouTube wants it per-video).
2. Verbally mention affiliate links/sponsorships in the VIDEO itself at the time of the plug ("quick note: this is an affiliate link, I may earn a commission if you sign up — no extra cost to you").
3. Use YouTube Studio's built-in "Paid promotion" checkbox on sponsored videos.
4. Review the FTC Endorsement Guides annually: https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/ftcs-endorsement-guides-what-people-are-asking
5. When in doubt: DISCLOSE. Over-disclosure is safe. Non-disclosure risks $43,792+ per violation in FTC fines.

## YouTube Community Guidelines Reminders

Don't:
- Mislead about the nature of content (clickbait that doesn't deliver is TOS violation)
- Hide sponsored content
- Use others' copyrighted content (see COPYRIGHT.md)
- Make harmful/dangerous content
- Coordinate deceptive activity (sub4sub, fake engagement)
- Post content that endangers children (COPPA compliance is mandatory — if content is for kids, mark it as MADE FOR KIDS; if not, mark as NOT MADE FOR KIDS)
