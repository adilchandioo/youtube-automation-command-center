# 💰 Monetization Playbook

YouTube channels don't have to live on AdSense alone. The best channels earn from 4+ streams.

---

## Revenue Streams (in order of activation)

### 1. Affiliate Marketing (ACTIVATE DAY 1 — don't wait for YPP!)
- Add affiliate links in description from DAY ONE
- Programs to join immediately:
  - **Amazon Associates** (easy approval, 1-3% commission, huge catalog)
  - **ShareASale** / **CJ Affiliate** / **Impact Radius** (big affiliate networks)
  - **Digistore24** / **ClickBank** (info products, high commissions $20-$200+)
  - **Direct SaaS affiliate programs** (most SaaS tools have an "Affiliates" link in footer — $20-$500 CPA per signup)
  - **Web hosting** (Hostinger, Bluehost pay $60-$150 per referral!)
  - **AI tools** (Jasper, Midjourney alternatives, etc. — hot space)
- Rule: Only recommend tools YOU actually use or have tested. Your audience trusts you; don't break it for $20.
- Disclose: "#ad" or "affiliate link" in description + verbal mention ("this is an affiliate link, I may earn a commission if you sign up — no extra cost to you")

### 2. YouTube AdSense (after YPP: 1k subs + 4k watch hours)
- Apply once you hit thresholds (takes 1–4 weeks for review)
- RPM varies by niche (finance $20-50, gaming $2-6)
- Don't rely on this alone — ad rates are volatile and YouTube takes 45%
- Use mid-roll ads on videos >8 min (place at natural breaks, don't spam)
- Turn ON all ad formats (skippable, overlay, shorts ads)
- Don't swear excessively or mention prohibited topics — triggers demonetization

### 3. Sponsorships & Brand Deals (after ~5k–10k subs)
- **How to get them**:
  - Have a public email in channel "About" section (e.g., "biz@yourchannel.com")
  - Join marketplaces: Grapevine, Famebit (rebranded YouTube BrandConnect), Upfluence, AspireIQ
  - Cold-email brands in your niche (template in `sponsorships/sponsor-pitch-email.txt`)
- **Pricing benchmark (2026)**:
  - 10k subs: $200-$500 per integration
  - 50k subs: $1,000-$3,000
  - 100k subs: $3,000-$8,000
  - $20-30 CPM for niche finance/tech
- Rules:
  - Only promote brands you'd genuinely recommend
  - Integrate sponsor NATURALLY (60-90 sec dedicated spot + end mention — not a hard cut)
  - Disclose verbally AND in description (#ad or #sponsored)
  - Never take a deal that forces you to lie about results

### 4. Digital Products (after ~5k subs + email list)
Highest margin (95-99%):
- **PDF guides / checklists** ($17-$47) — e.g., "30 AI Side Hustles PDF"
- **Templates** ($27-$97) — notion templates, script templates, thumbnail packs
- **Courses** ($97-$497) — deep dive course on your niche topic
- **Paid newsletter/community** ($5-$25/mo) — Discord/Skool community + monthly training
- **Coaching/consulting** ($100-$500/hr) — limited 1:1
- Launch when you get 10+ comments/emails a week asking for deeper help

### 5. Memberships / Patreon (after ~10k loyal fans)
- YouTube Memberships (built-in) or Patreon
- Offer tiers: $5/mo (early access + polls), $15/mo (monthly Q&A), $50/mo (1:1 call, resource pack)
- Good for channels with loyal repeat audiences (education, tutorials, behind-the-scenes)

### 6. Shorts/TikTok Creator Funds (small, but free money)
- YouTube Shorts Fund (ad revenue share post-2023 changes)
- Repost Shorts to TikTok — join TikTok Creator Program for direct payouts
- Instagram Reels Bonus (inconsistent, declining)
- Don't rely on these — pennies compared to other streams

### 7. Lead Gen / CPA Offers
- Pay-per-lead programs (e.g., "free quote" services, insurance, mortgages, solar, software trials)
- Payout $10-$100 PER LEAD (no sale required!)
- Works especially well in finance, insurance, SaaS free trials
- Use dedicated landing pages (track with UTM params)

---

## Revenue Diversification Rule

By the time you hit 10k subs, aim for revenue split:
- AdSense: 30-40%
- Affiliates: 30%
- Sponsorships: 20%
- Digital products: 10-20%

Never be >60% dependent on ONE revenue stream. YouTube can demonetize you overnight — you need owned revenue (email list, products) to survive.
