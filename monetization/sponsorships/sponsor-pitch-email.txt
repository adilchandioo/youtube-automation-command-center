# Sponsor Pitch Email Template
# Send to brands whose products you genuinely use and want to recommend.

SUBJECT: Partnership idea — {{BRAND_NAME}} × {{CHANNEL_NAME}} ({{N_SUBS}} subscribers)

Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

I'm {{YOUR_NAME}}, creator of {{CHANNEL_NAME}} on YouTube. We make videos about {{NICHE}} for an audience of {{N_SUBS}} active subscribers, with ~{{VIEWS_PER_MONTH}} monthly views.

Our audience is {{AVATAR_DESCRIPTION}} — exactly the customer {{BRAND_NAME}} is trying to reach.

I've been using {{PRODUCT_NAME}} personally for {{TIME_PERIOD}} and love it because {{GENUINE_REASON}}. I'd love to feature your tool in an upcoming video — and I have one next week about {{TOPIC}} where your product would fit perfectly.

Here's what I can offer:
- 60–90 second dedicated integration in a video on my main channel (~{{VIEWS_EXPECTED}} expected views in 30 days)
- 30-day pinned top comment with your link
- Link in description + end-screen card
- Optional: 1 dedicated Short pre/post publish (100k-300k expected additional views)

Stats from my last 5 integrations:
- Average CTR to sponsor link: {{CTR}}%
- Average conversions: {{CONVERSIONS}} per video
- Example case study: {{EXAMPLE_BRAND}} got {{EXAMPLE_RESULT}} from their integration with me.

Rate for this package: ${{RATE}} flat fee.
I'm also open to performance bonuses (% of sale) if you prefer a hybrid model.

If this sounds interesting, I'd love to jump on a quick 15-min call this week. Does {{DAY_1}} or {{DAY_2}} at {{TIME}} work for you?

If not the right fit, no worries at all — I'll keep recommending {{PRODUCT_NAME}} to my audience either way, since I genuinely use it.

Best,
{{YOUR_NAME}}
{{CHANNEL_NAME}}
{{PORTFOLIO_LINK}} (link to media kit / previous sponsors)
{{BUSINESS_EMAIL}}

## Follow-up (if no reply in 5 days):

Hi {{FIRST_NAME}}, just floating this back up in case it got buried. Happy to send my full media pack with audience demographics if helpful.

No pressure either way — just wanted to make sure you saw it!

Best,
{{YOUR_NAME}}

---

## Media Kit Stats to Prepare (attach PDF)
- Channel name + logo
- Sub count + monthly views
- Audience demographics (age, gender split, top countries, top interests) — from YouTube Analytics Audience tab
- Top 10 videos with view counts
- Previous brand partners (with logos)
- Average CTR on past integrations
- Rate card (integrations, dedicated Shorts, email send, package deals)
- Testimonials from past sponsors
