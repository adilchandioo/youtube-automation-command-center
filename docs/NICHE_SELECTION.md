# 🎯 Niche Selection Framework

> A bad niche = you work 10x harder for 1/10th the money. Pick right.

---

## Niche Scoring Rubric

Score each candidate niche 1–10 on each dimension. Multiply for final score. Pursue niches scoring **>350** (out of 600).

| Dimension | Weight | What to check |
|---|---|---|
| **CPM ($$ RPM)** | x2 | Finance/tech/biz/health/insurance/real estate = high ($15–$40 RPM). Comedy/gaming/vlogs = low ($2–$6). |
| **Evergreen-ness** | x2 | Will people search this in 3 years? "How to budget" = yes. "React to TikTok trend X" = no. |
| **Competition (lower = better)** | x1.5 | Search main keyword: are top videos from faceless channels you can beat, or from MrBeast/TV networks? |
| **Search volume** | x1.5 | TubeBuddy/VidIQ: does main keyword have >10k/mo searches? Are there 10+ long-tail variants? |
| **Content production ease** | x1 | Can you make 50+ videos using AI voice + B-roll? Avoid anything requiring your face/location/expensive gear. |
| **Affiliate/digital product potential** | x1.5 | Are there tools, courses, or products to recommend? Finance → credit cards, brokerages; Tech → SaaS affiliate $20–$200 CPA. |
| **Monetization friendliness** | x1 | Will YPP allow ads? True crime / controversial topics get demonetized. Stay advertiser-friendly. |
| **Your interest/knowledge** | x0.5 | You'll grind harder if you care. Not critical — VAs can execute — but helps for long-term. |

**Formula**: `Score = (CPM*2 + Evergreen*2 + (11-Competition)*1.5 + SearchVol*1.5 + Ease*1 + Affiliate*1.5 + YPP*1 + Interest*0.5) * 10`

## ✅ Recommended MVP Niches (already score well)

### Finance / Make Money Online
- **Angle examples**: "Side hustles for X", "Passive income ideas", "How to make $X/day online", "Millionaire habits", "Jobs that pay X without a degree"
- CPM: $20–$50
- Affiliates: Hostinger, Shopify, ClickBank, brokerages
- Watch-out: avoid "get rich quick" claims (YouTube demonetizes). Use "potential earnings", frame as education.

### Tech Tutorials / SaaS Reviews
- **Angle examples**: "X AI tool tutorial", "Best project management software 2026", "How to use ChatGPT for X", "Free alternatives to Photoshop"
- CPM: $15–$40
- Affiliates: SaaS affiliate programs ($20–$500 CPA per signup!)
- Watch-out: actually test tools you review, don't just regurgitate sales pages.

### Business / Case Studies
- **Angle examples**: "How X company started in a garage", "Why X brand failed", "Business lessons from billionaires", "How X grew to $1M"
- CPM: $20–$45
- Affiliates: Business courses, software, books (Amazon Associates)
- Great for long-form storytelling (12–20 min docs).

### Health (Adjacent — longevity, fitness hacks, men's/women's health)
- **Angle**: "Scientists say X adds 10 years to your life", "Drink this every morning for X", "Why you're always tired"
- CPM: $15–$40
- Watch-out: **never give medical advice**, always add disclaimer ("this is educational, consult a doctor"). Avoid prescription drug mentions.

### Productivity / Self-Improvement (with science)
- **Angle**: "Harvard study says X makes you 3x more productive", "Naval Ravikant's 5 rules for wealth", "Dark psychology tricks"
- CPM: $10–$25
- Affiliates: Books, courses, apps
- Low friction to produce — lots of stock B-roll available.

### History / Documentary (animated)
- **Angle**: "The untold story of X", "How the Roman Empire really fell", "Forgotten wars"
- CPM: $8–$18, but huge watch time (15–25 min avg view duration)
- Requires higher editing skill (maps, animations) but very defensible.

## ❌ Niches to AVOID (at least at MVP)

| Niche | Why avoid |
|---|---|
| Gaming / Let's Plays | Impossible to compete with big creators, super low CPM |
| Reaction videos | Copyright flags, low retention after trend dies |
| True crime (graphic) | Demonetization, age restriction risk |
| Compilation / "best of" clips | Copyright strikes (people sue) |
| News / Politics | Volatile, demonetized, age-restricted, drama |
| Crypto (in 2026 regulatory landscape) | Ad restrictions, scams flagged |
| Kids content (COPPA) | Heavily regulated, no personalized ads, huge fines if you mess up |
| Celebrity gossip | Copyright of likeness, defamation risk |
| Motivation with licensed music | Copyright strikes |

## Niche Validation Checklist (before committing)

Go through this for any niche candidate:

- [ ] Top 10 channels in the niche have at least one video with <100k subs but >1M views (proves new channels can pop)
- [ ] I can find 100+ video ideas in 1 hour (proves depth)
- [ ] TubeBuddy shows keyword "fair" or better difficulty for top terms
- [ ] At least 3 affiliate programs exist (Amazon, ShareASale, CJ, ClickBank, or niche-specific)
- [ ] I can produce a video in this niche using: AI voice + stock B-roll + captions (no on-camera needed)
- [ ] The niche doesn't violate YouTube advertiser-friendly guidelines
- [ ] CPM estimator (SocialBlade / TubeBuddy) shows >$10 RPM

## Spinoff Strategy

Once channel 1 in a niche hits 10k subs:
1. Launch a "Shorts-only" spinoff in same niche (clips + repurpose)
2. Launch a sub-niche channel (e.g. main = "side hustles" → sub = "AI side hustles" → sub = "side hustles for students")
3. Repeat winner niches in other languages (Spanish, Portuguese, Hindi — often lower competition, growing fast)

## Document It

When you pick a niche, create a file in `docs/channels/` named `CH-001-{niche-slug}.md` containing:
- Niche + sub-niche
- ICP avatar (1 paragraph)
- Top 10 competitors
- Top 20 seed video ideas
- Channel name candidates
- Branding direction (colors, voice type, pace)
- Affiliate programs to join
- Upload schedule (e.g. Mon/Wed/Fri 4pm ET)

See `docs/CHANNEL_SYSTEM.md` for the template.
