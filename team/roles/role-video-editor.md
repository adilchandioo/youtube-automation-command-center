# Role: Video Editor

**Reports to**: Founder / Channel Manager
**Rate**: $10–$20 per 8–12 min video (freelance) OR $4–$7/hr (retainer)
**Hours**: Part-time to start, 10–20 hrs/week; full-time possible at scale
**Location**: Remote, any timezone (but must be able to deliver within 24h turnaround)

---

## What You'll Do
- Edit 3–10 long-form videos per week (8–15 min each)
- Edit 10–30 Shorts per week (30–55 sec)
- Add auto-captions, B-roll, music, sound effects, transitions
- Follow channel brand guidelines (fonts, colors, style)
- Work from CapCut (preferred) or Premiere Pro
- Deliver exported MP4 + project file
- Take feedback well and implement quickly

## Required Skills
- 1+ years editing YouTube-style videos (portfolio required)
- Proficient in CapCut OR Premiere Pro OR DaVinci Resolve
- Good ear for pacing (know when to cut, when to linger)
- Eye for what B-roll matches what's being said
- English reading comprehension (to sync captions and understand script)
- Reliable internet + computer that can handle 1080p exports
- Ability to match a reference video style exactly
- Meets deadlines without excuses

## Nice-to-Haves
- Motion graphics / After Effects
- Color grading skills
- Experience with YouTube automation specifically
- Sound design skills
- Know how to do "MrBeast-style" zoom/clickbait edits for retention

## KPIs
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| On-time delivery | >95% |
| Revision requests per video | <2 |
| Videos edited per week | 5+ (long-form) |
| Average AVD on your edits | >40% |

## First 30 Days
- Week 1: Edit 3 test videos with heavy feedback
- Week 2: 5 videos with light feedback
- Week 3+: Hand off full editing responsibility with minimal oversight
