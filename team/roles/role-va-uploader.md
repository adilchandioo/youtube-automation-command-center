# Role: VA — Uploader & SEO Assistant

**Reports to**: Channel Manager / Founder
**Rate**: $3–$5/hr
**Hours**: 1–3 hrs/day, 5–7 days/week
**Location**: Remote (any — we follow audience timezone)

---

## What You'll Do
- Upload finished videos to YouTube Studio
- Fill in titles, descriptions, tags, playlists
- Schedule videos for correct publish times
- Set end screens and cards
- Manage Shorts upload + cross-post to TikTok/IG Reels
- Pin comments after publish
- Reply to comments (using templates + AI reply assist)
- Daily QA of next-day's scheduled videos
- Pull weekly stats into KPI tracker
- Flag issues (claims, strikes, glitches) immediately

## Required Skills
- Detail-oriented (critical — typos in titles are costly)
- Basic YouTube Studio knowledge (we train)
- Good written English
- Reliable internet
- Can follow SOPs EXACTLY
- Proactive communicator (flags problems before they blow up)
- Spreadsheet competency (Google Sheets)
- Trello/Notion basic usage

## KPIs
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Uploads on schedule | 100% |
| Mistakes per upload (typos, wrong tags, etc.) | <1 every 20 videos |
| Comments replied to within 1h of publish | 20+ per video |
| Daily checklist completion | 100% |
| Strikes/claims caught before publish | 100% |

## Training Provided
- SOP-001 Daily Publish Routine
- SOP-006 Copyright Safety
- Walkthrough video of YouTube Studio settings
- 3-day shadowing with founder before solo work

## What Will NOT Be Tolerated
- Missing a publish slot
- Uploading wrong video file / thumbnail
- Forgetting affiliate disclosure (FTC risk)
- Missing a strike/warning notification
- Not showing up without 24h notice

## Growth Path
Top performers can grow into Channel Manager role with higher pay ($800–$1,500/mo).
