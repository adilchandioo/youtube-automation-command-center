# Role: Script Writer

**Reports to**: Founder / Channel Manager
**Rate**: $5–$15 per 8–12 min script
**Hours**: Freelance, per-script (can do 5–10 scripts/week)
**Location**: Remote

---

## What You'll Do
- Write 3–7 long-form YouTube scripts per week (1500 words each)
- Write 10–20 Shorts scripts per week
- Research topics (use competitor videos, Reddit, Google Trends)
- Write in the channel's brand voice (training + reference scripts provided)
- Incorporate SEO keywords naturally
- Revise scripts based on feedback (2 rounds max per script)
- Provide metadata (title options, tags, thumbnail ideas) with each script

## Required Skills
- Native/fluent English
- 1+ year writing online (YouTube scripts, blog posts, copywriting)
- You WATCH YouTube — you understand what hooks people
- Storytelling ability (can turn dry info into narrative)
- Research skills (can fact-find quickly)
- Responsive to feedback, no ego
- Basic SEO understanding
- Ability to match a specific voice/tone (we give you examples)

## KPIs
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Scripts delivered per week | 5+ |
| Scripts approved on first draft | >60% |
| Avg CTR on your scripts | >7% |
| Avg AVD on your scripts | >40% |
| Revision rounds per script | <2 |

## Writing Style to Match
We'll give you:
- 3 example high-performing scripts
- Channel persona/avatar
- Vocabulary/phrase lists specific to the channel
- Words/phrases to avoid

Study them. Don't write generic AI-sounding content. Write like a friend talking.
