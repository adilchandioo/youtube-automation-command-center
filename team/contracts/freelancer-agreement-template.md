# INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

**Effective Date**: {{DATE}}

**Parties**:
- **Client**: {{YOUR_NAME_OR_COMPANY}}, ("Client" / "the Company")
- **Contractor**: {{CONTRACTOR_NAME}}, located at {{CONTRACTOR_ADDRESS}}, ("Contractor")

## 1. Services
Contractor agrees to perform the following services:
{{DESCRIPTION_OF_WORK}} (e.g., "Video editing services for Client's YouTube channels, as assigned via Trello, up to {{N}} videos per week").

## 2. Payment
- Rate: {{RATE}} (e.g., "$15 per finished 8-12 minute video")
- Payment schedule: Weekly, via {{PAYMENT_METHOD}} (Wise/PayPal/etc.)
- Payment terms: Net 7 (invoices paid within 7 days of submission)
- Contractor is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and benefits. No withholding.

## 3. Independent Contractor Status
- Contractor is NOT an employee, partner, or agent of Client.
- Contractor sets own hours and works using own equipment.
- Contractor may work for other clients, but may not work for direct YouTube competitors in the same niche during engagement (see Non-Compete below, if applicable).
- No benefits (health, retirement, paid leave, etc.) are provided.

## 4. Intellectual Property / Work-For-Hire
- All work product (scripts, edits, thumbnails, videos, voiceover) created by Contractor for Client is **work-made-for-hire** and the exclusive property of Client.
- Client retains all right, title, and interest in the work, including copyright.
- Contractor may not reuse, resell, publish, or share work product with third parties.
- Contractor may NOT include the work in their public portfolio without written permission from Client (to protect channel strategy).

## 5. Confidentiality
Contractor agrees to keep confidential:
- Channel niches, strategies, revenue data, traffic data
- SOPs, templates, prompts, playbooks in this repository
- Subscriber data, email lists
- Future launch plans
- Any information marked "confidential"

This obligation survives termination of the agreement.

## 6. Non-Disparagement
Both parties agree not to make defamatory or disparaging statements about each other publicly (social media, forums, YouTube, etc.).

## 7. Revisions
- Each deliverable includes up to {{N}} rounds of revisions at no extra charge.
- Additional revisions beyond that are billed at {{RATE_FOR_EXTRA_REVISIONS}}.

## 8. Deadline & Delivery
- Delivery deadlines are set per task in Trello/Notion.
- Late delivery without 24h prior notice: Client may deduct {{LATE_PENALTY_PERCENT}} from payment for each day late (reasonable exceptions allowed on a case-by-case basis).

## 9. Termination
- Either party may terminate this agreement with 7 days written notice (email).
- Client may terminate immediately (without notice) for:
  - Missed deadlines repeatedly
  - Plagiarism or copyright infringement by Contractor
  - Breach of confidentiality
  - Failure to deliver acceptable quality after 2 revision rounds
  - Any illegal activity
- On termination, Contractor delivers all in-progress work; Client pays for work completed up to termination date.

## 10. Originality & Copyright Compliance
- Contractor warrants all work is original or properly licensed for YouTube monetization.
- Contractor agrees to use only approved footage/music sources (per Client's SOP-006).
- Contractor will NOT use copyrighted material from other YouTubers, films, TV, music, etc.
- If a video receives a copyright claim or strike due to Contractor's unauthorized use of material, Contractor is responsible for correcting it at their own cost; repeated instances are grounds for immediate termination.

## 11. Indemnification
Each party indemnifies the other against claims arising from their own breach, negligence, or willful misconduct.

## 12. Dispute Resolution
- Parties will first attempt to resolve disputes amicably.
- If unresolved, disputes go to mediation before litigation.
- Governing law: {{YOUR_STATE_OR_COUNTRY_LAW}}.
