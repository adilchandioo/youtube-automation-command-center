## How We Work

### Communication
- We use Discord/Slack for daily chat
- All task-related communication happens in Trello/Notion card comments (not DMs!)
- Response expectation: within 12 hours on workdays
- If you're going to be late or miss a deadline, tell us EARLY (not after the fact)
- We respect time zones — no one expects you to respond at 3am

### Payments
- Payday: every Friday (or bi-weekly after trust is built)
- Payment method: Wise / PayPal
- You invoice us weekly with hours/deliverables
- Bonuses: $20–$50 for any video you work on that hits 100k+ views in 30 days
- Raises: at 90-day reviews based on KPI performance

### Tools We Use
- **Project management**: Trello / Notion
- **Files**: Google Drive
- **Chat**: Discord/Slack
- **Video editing**: CapCut (preferred) / Premiere Pro / DaVinci
- **Thumbnails**: Canva Pro (we provide access) + Midjourney
- **Voiceover**: ElevenLabs
- **Scripts**: Google Docs (shared folder)

### Quality Standards
We have a high bar but we're kind people. We give specific, actionable feedback — take it as help to make your work better, not criticism. If you don't understand something, ask.

For your first 10 pieces of work, expect detailed feedback on every single one. After that, feedback gets lighter as you learn our style.

### What Gets You Fired (fast)
- Plagiarism / stealing others' work
- Missing 2 deadlines without heads-up
- Rude/hostile communication
- Sharing our SOPs, scripts, or content with anyone outside the team
- Deliberately cutting corners on copyright/safety rules
- Lying about work done

### What Gets You Promoted / Raises
- Consistently hitting deadlines
- Quality that gets BETTER over time (not just staying the same)
- Proactively suggesting improvements
- Being easy to work with (low drama, solutions-focused)
- Videos you work on hitting high CTR/AVD
