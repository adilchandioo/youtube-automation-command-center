## Short FORMULA Variants

### Variant 1: THE TWIST (story format)
> 0-3s: "My boss fired me via email."
> 3-10s: "So I went home and started doing this weird thing online."
> 10-35s: [explain the thing]
> 35-45s: "Last month I made more money in 1 week than I made in 1 month at that job." [proof screenshot]
> 45-55s: "Full story is on my channel. Tap above."

### Variant 2: 3-ITEM LIST (fast cuts)
> 0-3s: "3 side hustles that pay $500+/week NO EXPERIENCE"
> 3-15s: "#1 — [10 sec explanation with B-roll]"
> 15-28s: "#2 — [10 sec]"
> 28-40s: "#3 — BEST ONE — [12 sec, more detail]"
> 40-50s: "Number 3 is the one I do personally. Tap above for the full tutorial."

### Variant 3: REACTION / CONTROVERSY
> 0-3s: [reacting to a shocking clip/stat/claim, face shocked]
> 3-15s: "Wait — is this REAL?..."
> 15-40s: [explain what's happening, why it's shocking, the real story]
> 40-50s: "The full breakdown is on my channel right now."
> 50-55s: "Follow, I expose this stuff daily."

### Variant 4: HOW-TO (fast tutorial)
> 0-3s: "How to make $100 today [step 1 is on screen]"
> 3-40s: fast screen recording doing the thing with captions
> 40-50s: [show result — e.g., PayPal payment received]
> 50-55s: "Full step-by-step is linked above."

## Caption/Text-on-Screen Rules (CRITICAL)

- 70% watch Shorts WITH SOUND OFF — text-on-screen is NON-NEGOTIABLE
- 2-3 words AT A TIME, centered lower third (below face, above subscribe bar)
- Bold font, white text with 4px black outline
- NEW text every 2-3 seconds (pattern interrupts for scrollers)
- HIGHLIGHT key numbers/words in yellow/red
- Caption EVERY sentence

## Audio for Shorts

- Voiceover slightly louder than long-form (-3dB)
- Trending audio (if relevant) at -24dB under VO
- Or: no music, just clear voice (works well for educational/tutorial Shorts)
- Add a subtle "whoosh" sound effect between scene cuts

## Export Settings

- 1080x1920, 9:16
- 30fps (60fps if lots of text/zooms)
- MP4 H.264
- Target file size <100MB for quick upload

## Hashtags (description)
