# Long-Form Video Script Template

Copy this into Google Docs / your script editor and fill in. 150 words per target minute.

---

```
[METADATA]
Channel: CH-XXX
Episode: #___
Title A:
Title B:
Title C:
Primary keyword:
Secondary keywords:
Thumbnail idea A:
Thumbnail idea B:
Target length: ___ min
Target publish date:

[CHAPTERS]
0:00 Intro / hook
1:30 Point 1 — ____
4:00 Point 2 — ____
6:30 Point 3 — ____
9:00 Point 4 — ____
10:30 CTA / outro
```

---

## [0:00-0:30] HOOK

**[OPEN on extreme close-up face reaction / B-roll of payoff / money shot]**

> [WRITE THE HOOK HERE — 1-3 sentences. Make them need to keep watching.]
>
> Examples:
> - "Last week I made $8,492 from a side hustle I spend 2 hours a week on. And nobody on the internet is talking about it."
> - "Stop applying for remote jobs. If you're watching this after 2025, there are 3 secrets nobody tells you about getting hired."
> - "There's a reason 97% of side hustlers never make more than $100. It's not effort. It's not skill. It's what they're selling."

> By the end of this video, you'll know exactly [what payoff] — and [bonus payoff]. Stick with me, because [#4 / the last one] is going to surprise you.

---

## [0:30-1:00] INTRO

> If we haven't met, I'm [host persona name / "your host"] — on this channel we [channel positioning: "cut through the BS and show you what actually works to make money online"].
>
> If that sounds like something you want more of, do me a quick favor: hit the like button and subscribe. It takes 2 seconds and it genuinely helps the channel grow so I can keep making videos like this.
>
> Now — let's get into it. And wait till you see point number [X]. That's the one [I personally use / made me $X / changed everything for my students].

---

## [1:00-___] POINT 1 (2-3 min)

**[B-ROLL: set scene — relevant establishing footage]**

> [Topic sentence: state point 1 clearly]
>
> [Evidence/stat: "According to [source], [stat]. In fact, [second stat]."]
>
> [Story/example: "Here's what this looks like in real life. A guy named [name] [story]. He started by [first step] and in [time] he was [result]."]
>
> [Specific how-to / actionable steps: "Here's exactly how you do this. First, go to [website]. Then, [step]. What you want to do is [detail]. Most people mess this up by [mistake] — don't do that."]
>
> **[SCREEN RECORDING: walk through the step if applicable]**
>
> [Mini payoff / result: "If you do this correctly, you can expect [realistic result] in [timeframe]."]
>
> [Transition: "Okay, that was point one. Point two? It's where the real money starts."]

**[TEXT POP-UP: "POINT 1 — KEY TAKEAWAY" + 1 sentence]**

---

## [Repeat for POINTS 2, 3, 4+]

Same structure:
- Topic sentence
- Evidence/stat
- Story/example
- How-to / actionable steps
- Payoff
- Transition / mini-hook

---

## [MID-VIDEO RETENTION HOOK at ~50% mark]

> Quick pause. If you're getting value from this so far, do me a favor —
> **[ZOOM IN on face or key visual]**
>
> Smash that subscribe button. I post 3 videos a week on [niche] — and I don't waste your time with fluffy advice. I give you the exact steps. So if you want more of that, hit subscribe, ring the bell, and you'll get notified the second I drop a new one.
>
> Alright — let's keep going. And just so you know, the next one? It's the most controversial thing I've ever said on this channel. You don't want to miss it.

---

## FINAL POINT — THE BEST ONE

Structure the same but give it MORE time, MORE specific details, and a STRONGER story. End with a punch.

> Now, here's the big one. The one that separates people who actually make money from people who just watch videos about making money.
>
> [Best story, most specific how-to, clearest example.]

---

## OUTRO / CTA (45 sec)

> Alright, let's wrap this up.
>
> Today we covered:
> - [Point 1 in 1 sentence]
> - [Point 2 in 1 sentence]
> - [Point 3 in 1 sentence]
> - [Point 4 in 1 sentence]
>
> **[B-ROLL: montage of points if available]**
>
> Here's what I want you to do RIGHT NOW: pick ONE of these — just one — and take action on it before the end of the day. Don't watch another video. Don't save it for later. Open a new tab, go do step one. That's the difference between people who succeed and people who don't.
>
> If you got value, hit like. It helps other people find this video. Subscribe for more [niche] content every week.
>
> Drop a comment down below — which of these are you going to try first? I read every comment in the first hour after I post, so I'll see you down there.
>
> **[AFFILIATE PLUG if applicable, 10 sec]:**
> "And if you want to [fast-track result / do what I do], I put together a [free PDF / full course / template] — link is the first thing in the description. It's [free / $X], and it walks you through [benefit]."
>
> **[END SCREEN: related videos + subscribe pop in]**
>
> On [next upload day], I'm dropping a video about [next video teaser — make it juicy]. You don't want to miss that one. Hit the bell so you're notified.
>
> See you then. Now go do the work.

**[FADE OUT with outro music + channel logo]**
