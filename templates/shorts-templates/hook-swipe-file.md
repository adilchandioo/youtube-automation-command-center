## Tips for Using Hooks

1. **OPEN MID-SENTENCE** — don't say "hey guys what's up"
2. Say the hook in 1 breath (under 3 seconds)
3. MATCH the visual to the hook (if you say "I got banned", look frustrated / hold up a phone showing the ban)
4. Use ZOOM IN on your face for emphasis
5. Add a TEXT OVERLAY of the key number/claim for silent viewers
6. TEST different hooks for the same content (cut multiple Shorts with different opens)
7. After the hook, CONTINUE immediately — don't pause or say "welcome back"
8. Steal from other niches (a finance hook works in health if you adapt numbers)
