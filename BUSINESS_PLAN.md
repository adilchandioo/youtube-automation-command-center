# 💼 BUSINESS PLAN — YouTube Automation Command Center

## 1. Executive Summary

**Business Name**: YouTube Automation Command Center (YACC) — internal op system; public-facing brands are per-channel.
**Model**: Portfolio of faceless/leveraged YouTube channels across evergreen niches, monetized via ads, affiliates, sponsorships, and digital products.
**Goal**: Build a 10+ channel network generating $20k–$100k MRR within 12 months, with the founder working <10 hrs/week.

**Why now**: YouTube is the 2nd largest search engine, short-form (Shorts) gives new channels free algorithmic distribution, and AI tools (GPT-4, ElevenLabs, Runway, Descript) collapsed production cost from ~$500/video to ~$5–$20/video. The window is wide open but closing as more operators enter.

---

## 2. Niche Strategy

We focus on **high-CPM, evergreen, information-dense** niches where viewers watch long (high AVD) and advertisers pay dearly.

**Tier 1 Niches (start here)** — CPM $15–$50:
- Personal finance / make-money-online / investing
- Tech tutorials & SaaS reviews
- Business / entrepreneurship case studies
- Health optimization (men's/women's specific, longevity)
- Software tutorials & "how to use X"

**Tier 2 Niches (expand after win)** — CPM $8–$20:
- Psychology / human behavior / dark psychology
- History documentaries (animated)
- True crime (caution on demonetization)
- Luxury / "how the rich live"
- Productivity / self-improvement

**Tier 3 Niches (avoid at first)** — CPM $2–$8, high competition:
- Gaming, comedy, vlogs, general fitness, music compilations

See `docs/NICHE_SELECTION.md` for the scoring rubric.

## 3. Target Audience (ICP)

Each channel targets a single avatar. Example for a finance channel:

> **"Restless Raj"** — 22–38, male or female, college-educated or self-taught, stuck at $40–$80k/year job, watches YouTube for side-hustle ideas, wants to escape 9–5 within 2 years, buys courses/software on credit card if it promises escape. Buys through affiliate links if content is trustworthy.

We tailor scripts, hooks, and products to this avatar. No "for everyone" content.

- **LTV per video ≈ $1,500 on $12 spend** = **125x ROI** on winners
- Losers (under 10k views) lose ~$12; winners carry the portfolio.

This is why the game is volume + quality + iteration.

## 8. Go-to-Market & Growth

### Channel launch formula
1. First 24 hours: post 3 videos, 5 Shorts cut from them
2. First week: daily upload (1 video + 2 Shorts)
3. First 30 days: 1 long-form every other day, 2 Shorts daily
4. Pin comment with hook + engagement question + lead magnet
5. Cross-post Shorts to TikTok & IG Reels (link back)
6. Answer every comment for first 30 days (algo signal)

### Growth levers
- **CTR optimization** (thumbnail A/B testing — 5–15% swings)
- **AVD/Retention** (first 30 seconds, mid-roll hooks, pacing)
- **Series/playlists** (binge watch sessions = session time signal)
- **Shorts-to-long-form funnel** (Shorts preview, end screen to long form)
- **Collaborations** with same-niche channels at similar size

## 9. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| YouTube demonetization / TOS strike | Read `legal/COPYRIGHT.md`; original content only; no reused footage; multiple channels diversify risk |
| Channel ban | Don't put all eggs in one channel/niche; backup accounts; export all data weekly |
| AI voice detection / "low value" flag | Use high-quality ElevenLabs voices, add B-roll, captions, original editing — don't just AI-slop |
| VA quality drops | SOPs + example library + scorecard review every 10 videos |
| CPM volatility (recession, etc.) | Multiple revenue streams (affiliates/products don't fluctuate like ads) |
| Burnout | Automate fast, hire fast, batch-work, time-block 90-minute days |
